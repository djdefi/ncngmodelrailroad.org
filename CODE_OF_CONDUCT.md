# Code of Conduct

The N.C.N.G. Historical Model Railroad is a volunteer organization dedicated to preserving railroad history. We welcome contributions from everyone who shares that goal.

## Our Standards

- **Be respectful** — Treat fellow contributors with courtesy, whether online or in person
- **Be constructive** — Focus feedback on the work, not the person
- **Be patient** — Many contributors are volunteers learning as they go
- **Be inclusive** — We welcome people of all backgrounds and skill levels

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate at our public events

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please email **info@ncngmodelrailroad.org**. All reports will be handled respectfully and confidentially.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, and any communication channels related to this website.
