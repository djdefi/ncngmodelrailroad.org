aboutText: |
  The Nevada County Narrow Gauge Railroad Historical Model Railroad operates a detailed On3 scale model railroad at the Nevada County Fairgrounds in Grass Valley, California.

  We are an all-volunteer 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, incorporated in 1986, dedicated to preserving the memory of the original Nevada County Narrow Gauge Railroad that once served the mining communities of Nevada City, Grass Valley, and Colfax.
historyText: |
  The Nevada County Narrow Gauge Railroad operated from 1876 to 1942, serving the gold mining communities of Nevada County, California. Our model railroad recreates this historic line in On3 scale (O scale with 3-foot narrow gauge track).

  The model features DCC (Digital Command Control) for realistic train operations, detailed scenery representing the Sierra Nevada foothills, and even a live train cam for remote viewing.
