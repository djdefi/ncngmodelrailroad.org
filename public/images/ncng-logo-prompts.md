## 🎯 PROMPT 1 — Circular Badge (Best for: hats, patches, stickers)

Design a professional circular emblem logo for a nonprofit model railroad club. The logo should be print-ready for embroidery and screen printing.

**Text (must be legible and spelled exactly):**
- Top arc: "NEVADA COUNTY NARROW GAUGE"
- Center large: "N.C.N.G."
- Below center: "HISTORICAL MODEL RAILROAD"
- Bottom: "EST. 1986 • GRASS VALLEY, CA"

**Imagery:**
- A detailed side-profile silhouette of a vintage 1800s narrow gauge Baldwin steam locomotive (similar to NCNG Engine #5 "Tahoe") crossing a tall wooden trestle bridge
- Sierra Nevada pine trees and mountain ridgeline in the background
- Narrow gauge railroad tracks

**Style:**
- Vintage heritage badge, clean vector illustration
- Maximum 4 colors: dark maroon red (#8B0000), dark gold (#B8860B), black, and off-white/cream
- Bold serif lettering, evenly spaced
- No gradients — flat color only (for screen printing)
- High contrast so it reads clearly at 3 inches wide
- Transparent or solid background versions

**Do NOT include:** photorealism, 3D effects, drop shadows, thin hairline details that won't print

## 🖨️ PRODUCTION NOTES

After generating with AI, to make print-ready:

1. **Vectorize**: Use Adobe Illustrator Image Trace, Vectorizer.ai, or Inkscape "Trace Bitmap"
2. **Color separate**: Reduce to exact Pantone spot colors for screen printing
   - Maroon → Pantone 1815 C
   - Gold → Pantone 7555 C  
   - Forest Green → Pantone 350 C
3. **File formats needed**:
   - SVG/AI (vector) for screen printing & vinyl cutting
   - PNG 300dpi with transparent background for digital
   - DST/PES for embroidery (use embroidery digitizing service)
4. **Test at actual size** before ordering — print it on paper and hold it on the hat/shirt
5. **Embroidery rule of thumb**: No detail smaller than 1mm, no text smaller than 6mm tall
